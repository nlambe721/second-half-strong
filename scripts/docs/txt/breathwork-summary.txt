Episode Overview Summary
Most men focus on workouts.
Many focus on nutrition.
Some focus on supplements.
Very few focus on breathing.
In this powerful presentation, Funk Roberts breaks down why breathwork may be one of the
most underutilized tools for improving physical health, mental health, recovery, stress
management, focus, hormonal balance, and overall performance after 40.
Drawing from his experiences as a professional athlete, martial artist, fitness coach, and
survivor of a life-threatening lung condition, Funk explains how diaphragmatic breathing, deep
breathing, tactical breathing, and power breathing can dramatically improve how men recover
physically and mentally.
The presentation explores the science behind the sympathetic and parasympathetic nervous
systems, the connection between chronic stress and declining testosterone, and how simple
breathing techniques can help men improve focus, reduce anxiety, lower stress, and make
better decisions under pressure.
Most importantly, Funk reframes breathwork not as something "woo-woo," but as a practical
performance and recovery tool that every man should have in his toolbox.
