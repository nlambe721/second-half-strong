🔥 The 7-Minute “Sleep Reset” Protocol
(For Men Over 40 Who Wake Up at Night & Struggle to Fall Back Asleep)
Goal: This quick & effective protocol is designed to calm the nervous system, reset melatonin production, and guide you back into deep sleep within minutes.
🚨 Why You Wake Up & Can’t Fall Back Asleep
🔹 Cortisol Spike – Stress hormone wakes you up & keeps your mind racing.
🔹 Blood Sugar Drop – Low blood sugar causes adrenaline release.
🔹 Poor Sleep Environment – Light, temperature, or discomfort disrupts deep sleep.
🔹 Overthinking & Anxiety – Brain stays in problem-solving mode instead of rest mode.
💡 Solution: The 7-Minute Sleep Reset Protocol helps you shift back into sleep mode FAST.
🔥 The 7-Minute “Sleep Reset” Protocol
Step 1: STOP Looking at the Clock ⏰ (0:00 - 0:10 seconds)
🔹 Looking at the clock triggers stress and keeps you awake longer.
🔹 Instead, accept that you woke up & move into reset mode.
💡 Mindset Shift: "It’s okay—I’m going to use this time to get even deeper sleep."
Step 2: 4-7-8 Breathing Method (1 Minute)
✅ Inhale for 4 seconds
✅ Hold for 7 seconds
✅ Exhale for 8 seconds
🔹 This shifts your nervous system into relaxation mode (lowers cortisol & blood pressure).
🔥 Why It Works: Activates the parasympathetic nervous system (rest & digest mode), signaling your brain that it’s time to sleep.
Step 3: Full-Body Relaxation Scan (1 Minute)
✅ Mentally scan your body from head to toe, releasing tension.
✅ Focus on relaxing your jaw, shoulders, and lower back first.
✅ Breathe slowly & deeply, letting each muscle melt into relaxation.
🔥 Why It Works: Many men carry unconscious muscle tension, which triggers wake-ups.
Step 4: "Legs-Up Pose" Against the Wall (2 Minutes)
✅ Lay on your back & place your legs up against the wall (or rest them on a pillow).
✅ Hold for 2 minutes while focusing on slow breathing.
✅ This reduces blood flow to the brain, which lowers anxiety & cortisol.
🔥 Why It Works: Forces the nervous system to shift from "fight-or-flight" to relaxation mode.
Step 5: The "Reverse Sleep Countdown" (2 Minutes)
✅ Start at 100 and count backward slowly (100... 99... 98...).
✅ Imagine yourself sinking into the bed with each number.
✅ If your mind drifts, gently bring it back to counting.
🔥 Why It Works:
Distracts the brain from overthinking (removes anxiety about falling back asleep).
Slows brain wave activity, bringing the mind into a pre-sleep state.
Step 6: BONUS – Quick Sleep Reset Drink (If Needed)
💡 If you wake up due to hunger, blood sugar drops, or stress, drink:
🔹 Sleep Tonic Recipe:
✔ ½ teaspoon Himalayan salt + 1 teaspoon raw honey + warm water
✔ OR Chamomile / Valerian Root Tea
🔥 Why It Works:
Salt + honey stabilize blood sugar & prevent cortisol spikes.
Herbal tea contains natural compounds that relax the nervous system.
🚀 Summary: The 7-Minute “Sleep Reset” Protocol

🕒 1:00 - 2:00 min – Full-body relaxation scan (release tension).
🕒 2:00 - 4:00 min – Legs-up pose to lower cortisol & shift to deep relaxation.
🕒 4:00 - 6:00 min – Reverse countdown from 100 (forces brain into sleep mode).
🕒 6:00 - 7:00 min – BONUS: Quick sleep reset drink (only if needed).
