A Daily Mental Recovery Blueprint for Men Over 40
Phase 1: Learn to Breathe Properly (Week 1)
Daily Practice
5 Minutes
Diaphragmatic Breathing
1. Place one hand on chest
2. Place one hand on belly
3. Inhale through the nose
4. Expand the belly first
5. Exhale slowly
Goal
Train the diaphragm.
Create awareness.
Phase 2: Add Deep Breathing (Week 2)
Daily Practice
10 Minutes
Pattern:
● Inhale 4 seconds
● Hold 2 seconds
● Exhale 6 seconds

● Hold 2 seconds
Repeat for 10 minutes.
Goal
Improve relaxation.
Increase oxygen exchange.
Lower stress.
Phase 3: Build Tactical Focus (Week 3)
Box Breathing Protocol
Repeat:
● Inhale 4
● Hold 4
● Exhale 4
● Hold 4
For 5–10 minutes.
Use before:
● Important meetings
● Difficult conversations
● High-pressure situations
Goal
Train calm under pressure.

30 deep power breaths
